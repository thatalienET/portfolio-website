Hi!



I'm Alex, a motion designer and animator.


I bring determination and a drive for excellence to every project, big or small. I have a keen focus on developing playful and fun designs and animations and I am passionate about making good ideas come alive. I firmly believe that every project needs a great idea as much as excellent execution.


I love projects that break barriers and challenge me to problem-solve a unique solution. In every new project, I put the best of myself forward, combining fresh design with dynamic animations.



Software I use


I use the Adobe suite daily, primarily After Effects, Illustrator and Photoshop. I also use Premiere for editing and sound and InDesign for assembling storyboards for presentation to clients.


I am currently learning 3D design and animation in Cinema4D (Using Redshift as my rendering engine). I recently completed the School of Motion Cinema 4D Ascent course and have received an Intermediate 3D Animation certificate.
