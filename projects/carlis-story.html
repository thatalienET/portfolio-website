This project was a work of pure passion.


I was entusted by the charity Homeless Oxfordshire to tell Carli's real story. Given the tough and personal nature of the story I knew I would be dealing with, I wanted to use a style that captures the reality of life and its imperfections. This intention led me to chose a hand drawn style and to use Carlis actual voice as narration.
